{\bf (b)}
\begin{align*}
    P &= IV \\
    2000 &= I(115) \\
    \implies I &= \frac{400}{23}\text{A}.
\end{align*}
Thus,
\begin{align*}
    P &= I^2R \\
    2000 &= \left(\frac{400}{23}\right)^2 \cdot R \\
    \implies R &= \frac{529}{80} \\
    &\approx 6.1\Omega.
\end{align*}
